# ons: Download Data from the 'Office for National Statistics'

Provides functions to download and tidy statistical data published by
the 'Office for National Statistics' <https://www.ons.gov.uk>. Covers
GDP, inflation (CPI, CPIH, RPI), unemployment, employment, wages, trade,
retail sales, house prices, productivity, population, and public sector
finances. Most series are fetched from the 'ONS' website using its CSV
time series endpoint. House price data is sourced from 'HM Land
Registry' <https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/land-registry>.
Data is cached locally between sessions.
